7 Taylor Swift Songs that Mention Cities

This concept is brought on by Taylor Swift’s extensive discography. The artist has traveled the world and her songs reflect that, as several are tied to different locations that were poignant in the artist’s life or career. Here we are breaking down my seven favorite Taylor Swift songs that mention a city.

Welcome to New York

Album: 1989 (TV) This song features New York City, New York.

London Boy

Album: Lover This song features London, United Kingdom.

The Last Great American Dynasty

Album: Folklore This song features St. Louis, Missouri.

Dorothea

Album: Evermore This song features Tulupo, Mississippi.

The Very First Night

Album: Red (TV) This song features Los Angelos, California.

Paris

Album: Midnights This song features Paris, France.

Florida!!!

Album: The Tortured Poets Department This song features Destin, Florida.
